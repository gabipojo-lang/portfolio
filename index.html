About Me

I am Gabriela, a pre-business student at Brigham Young University pursuing a Bachelor of Science with a focus on Strategic Management. Originally from Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, I completed my Associate of Science in Social Media Marketing at Ensign College before transferring to BYU.

My background spans psychology, international services, and direct sales, giving me a lens on business that combines behavioral insight with strategic thinking. I am actively involved in the Management Consulting Association, Strategy Society, and Women in Strategy Association at BYU.
